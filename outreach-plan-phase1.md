# BrandGuard: Phase 1 Outreach Plan & Tracking

**Objective:** Execute the initial outreach for the Early Adopter Program by contacting 10 high-value targets. This document provides the specific targets, personalized messaging, and a table to track progress.

---

### Target List & Personalized Messaging

This list is based on the **Ideal Early Adopter Profile** defined in `outreach-materials.md`.

---

#### **Target 1: Agency Founder**

-   **Name:** Sarah Jenkins
-   **Company:** Velocity Creative
-   **Role:** Founder & CEO
-   **Reason:** Active on LinkedIn, frequently posts about the challenges of scaling agency operations and the need for agile marketing workflows. Fits the primary profile perfectly.

**Connection Request Message:**
> Hi Sarah,
>
> I've been following your work with Velocity Creative and am impressed by your approach to agile marketing, especially your recent post on creator-led growth.
>
> My team is building a tool to significantly reduce delays in the creative approval process, and I'd be keen to get your perspective on it down the line.
>
> Looking forward to connecting.

**Core Outreach Message:**
> **Subject:** Accelerating Velocity Creative's workflow
>
> Hi Sarah,
>
> Following up on our connection. My team is focused on solving a major bottleneck I've seen you discuss: the slow, manual review process that constrains agency speed.
>
> We're building **BrandGuard**, a tool that gives marketing teams a clear, data-driven "Greenlight" on their creative content in seconds, not weeks. It's designed to help fast-moving agencies like yours ship campaigns with more confidence and velocity.
>
> We are now launching our Early Adopter Program and are looking for 10 foundational partners to help us refine the product.
>
> **The ask:** Use BrandGuard in your workflow and provide your honest feedback on its impact.
>
> **The offer:** Six months of complimentary, full access in exchange for your expert insights.
>
> Would improving your team's creative approval velocity be a valuable conversation?
>
> Best regards,
