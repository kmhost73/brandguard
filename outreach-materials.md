# BrandGuard Early Adopter Program: Outreach Materials

**Objective:** Identify and onboard 10 foundational partners to provide critical feedback and help shape the future of the product.

---

### Ideal Early Adopter Profile

We are looking for innovative leaders in marketing who feel constrained by current workflows.

-   **Primary:** Founder/Partner at a growth-focused marketing or creative agency (5-20 employees). They understand the opportunity cost of time spent on manual review cycles.
-   **Secondary:** Senior freelance growth marketers specializing in DTC, influencer, or creator campaigns. Their reputation is built on delivering results with speed and precision.
-   **Psychographics:**
    -   Active on LinkedIn/Twitter.
    -   Publicly discusses marketing speed, agility, and the creator economy.
    -   Expresses frustration with bureaucracy or slow approval processes.
    -   Values tools that provide a clear competitive advantage.

---

### Phase 1: LinkedIn Connection Request

**Goal:** Establish a professional connection without a hard pitch.

**Template:**

> Hi [First Name],
>
> I've been following your work with [Company/Client or recent post topic] and am impressed by your approach to [mention specific area, e.g., growth marketing, creator partnerships].
>
> My team is building a tool to significantly reduce delays in the creative approval process, and I'd be keen to get your perspective on it down the line.
>
> Looking forward to connecting.

---

### Phase 2: The Core Outreach (LinkedIn/Email)

**Goal:** Clearly articulate the value proposition and the invitation to the early adopter program.

**Template:**

> **Subject (Email):** Accelerating your creative workflow
>
> Hi [First Name],
>
> Following up on our connection. My team is focused on solving a major bottleneck for marketing teams: the slow, manual, and often ambiguous pre-publication review process.
>
> We're building **BrandGuard**, a tool that gives marketing teams a clear, data-driven "Greenlight" on their creative content in seconds, not weeks. It's designed to help fast-moving teams ship campaigns with confidence and velocity.
>
> We are now launching our Early Adopter Program and are looking for 10 foundational partners to help us refine the product.
>
> **The ask:** Use BrandGuard in your workflow and provide your honest feedback on its impact.
>
> **The offer:** Six months of complimentary, full access in exchange for your expert insights.
>
> Would improving your team's creative approval velocity be a valuable conversation?
>
> Best regards,
>
> [Your Name/Signature]

---

### Phase 3: Follow-Up (If no response in 5 business days)

**Goal:** A single, polite, and professional follow-up.

**Template:**

> Hi [First Name],
>
> Just a brief follow-up on my previous message. If slow approval cycles are a challenge you're currently facing, I'd be happy to schedule a short call to show you how BrandGuard is addressing it.
>
> If the timing isn't right, no problem at all.
>
> All the best,
